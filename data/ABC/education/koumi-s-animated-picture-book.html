Koumi's Animated Picture Book

Koumi, a 6-year-old playful girl, observes the animals of the world in her picture book and takes you to meet them on every continent! An edutainment program to satisfy the curiosity of all kids.

Duration: 5 minutes 2 seconds5m

You live in cold regions where it snows in winter time. During the warmer seasons, you are brown on top and white underneath. And then, during winter, your fur turns totally white!
