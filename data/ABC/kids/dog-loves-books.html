Dog Loves Books

Dog loves books. He loves the smell of them; he loves the sound of them. But most of all, he loves the stories inside them!

Episodes

Dog Loves Robots

Duration: 7 minutes 1 second7m

Dog and Pug meet a great inventor on his space lab. The professor shows them all his robots but fails to realise that he has upset his very first creation. When Dog and Pug point this out, the professor realises his mistake.

This episode was published 17 hours ago, available until 7:33am on 29 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves Heroes

Duration: 7 minutes 7m

In a comic book world Dog and Pug meet two hapless super heroes who try to out do each other to save the city from their new visitors. The little girl they meet prefers Pug's Power Pug and he becomes the hero of the story.

This episode was published 2 days ago, available until 7:33am on 28 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves Monsters

Duration: 7 minutes 7m

Dog and Pug visit a spooky castle. They meet a princess who has no friends. Fortunately they find a baby dragon who has been making scary noises and when they are introduced to each other they become the best of friends.

This episode was published 3 days ago, available until 7:33am on 27 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves Snark

Duration: 7 minutes 1 second7m

Dog and Pug go on an adventure and they meet a Pirate Captain on a desert island. He is Hunting the Snark. Our friends realise that they should protect the creature, and develop a game of hide and seek.

This episode was published 4 days ago, available until 7:33am on 26 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves Kites

Duration: 7 minutes 7m

Pug makes a kite but he doesn't know how to fly it. He and Dog go to China where they meet two squabbling Pandas looking for bamboo shoots. Pug and his kite get blown up in the air and he lands in a bamboo forest.

This episode was published 5 days ago, available until 7:33am on 25 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves Diaries

Duration: 7 minutes 7m

Dog and Pug are trying to remember what they did yesterday. Their diary tells them that they found Granny Pug's lunchbox. They find her on the other side of a ravine so the neighbouring birds take them across the river.

This episode was published 6 days ago, available until 7:33am on 24 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves Hide And Seek

Duration: 7 minutes 7m

Dog and Pug go on an adventure and meet Camille the Chameleon who's very good at hiding. She is also very shy and when they hold a party no-one sees her. When they find another shy chameleon, Leon, the two can dance together.

This episode was published 7 days ago, available until 7:33am on 23 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves Billy Goat Gruff

Duration: 7 minutes 7m

Pug needs help mending his new see-saw. On their adventure our friends meet a Troll who guards an old bridge so that the goats cannot cross it. Dog persuades the Troll to build a new strong bridge.

This episode was published 1 week ago, available until 7:33am on 22 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves A Race

Duration: 7 minutes 7m

Pug is being competitive when he cleans the bookshop. He and Dog join a race in Australia. They help brother and sister Kangaroos who are very competitive, but get stuck in the mud.

This episode was published 1 week ago, available until 7:33am on 21 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves Questions And Answers

Duration: 7 minutes 7m

Pug starts asking Dog for the answers to life's big questions. They go on an adventure with Granny Pug who shows them that there are not always simple answers to difficult questions.

This episode was published 1 week ago, available until 7:33am on 20 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves Sleepy Time Rhymes

Duration: 6 minutes 58 seconds6m

Pug tries to remember an old nursery rhyme. He and Dog go to a fair where they meet the cat and the fiddle, the cow that jumps over the moon and the dish that ran away with the spoon.

This episode was published 2 weeks ago, available until 7:33am on 19 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves Dog Loves Books

Duration: 7 minutes 7m

Dog finds the original book that starts all of his adventures. He shows Pug how he started the bookshop and how he read all the books so that he could recommend them to each customer.

This episode was published 2 weeks ago, available until 7:33am on 18 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves Dinosaurs

Duration: 7 minutes 7m

Dog and Pug go an adventure underground where they meet Granny Pug. They slide down a tunnel to the centre of the earth. When they arrive, they find some very friendly dinosaurs.

This episode was published 2 weeks ago, available until 7:33am on 17 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves Record Breakers

Duration: 7 minutes 7m

Pug wants to break a world record. Dog suggests they go to the North Pole so that Pug can be the first pug to reach the Pole. They have some help from a Walrus but when they arrive, Granny Pug has got there first.

This episode was published 2 weeks ago, available until 7:33am on 16 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves Balancing

Duration: 7 minutes 7m

Pug needs help balancing on his skateboard. He and Dog arrive at the circus and meet the Great Splendina. She believes she cannot swing from the trapeze but she rescues Dog from a fall and helps Pug walk on the tightrope.

This episode was published 2 weeks ago, available until 7:33am on 15 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves An Anthology

Duration: 7 minutes 7m

Dog wants some high adventure and so he and Pug visit several different stories. Each time they arrive in their new story, they find that they are being chased by various characters. Mrs Honey Bunny comes to their rescue.

This episode was published 2 weeks ago, available until 7:33am on 14 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves Stars

Duration: 7 minutes 7m

Pug wants to get closer to the stars, having looked at the night sky from the roof. On their travels they meet Granny Pug who takes them on her spaceship. After seeing galaxies and shooting stars, they return to earth.

This episode was published 2 weeks ago, available until 7:33am on 13 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves Amazing Facts

Duration: 7 minutes 7m

Dog and Pug are talking facts and they go on an adventure to check one of Pug's facts. In the Fact Wherehouse they meet Wow the Owl. They get lost but devise a new way to finding their way out and solving Pug's problem.

This episode was published 3 weeks ago, available until 7:33am on 12 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves Don Quixote

Duration: 7 minutes 7m

Pug needs a story about how to be brave. Dog and Pug meet the Spanish knight Don Quixote and his partner Sancho Panza. Pug needs saving and Don Quixote defeats the giant windmills so Pug can now confront the hairy spider.

This episode was published 3 weeks ago, available until 7:33am on 11 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves A Quest

Duration: 7 minutes 7m

Dog and Pug help Doris the Donkey on her quest to become a unicorn. She has to pass three tests if she wants to succeed. However, Doris figures out that she is much better staying as a donkey and not being a unicorn.

This episode was published 3 weeks ago, available until 7:33am on 10 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves A Small Adventure

Duration: 7 minutes 7m

Dog loses his voice and Pug has to read a book. Pug goes on an adventure with the bookshop mice. They enter the body of Dog and find his heart and tummy although Pug loses the bookshop mice.

This episode was published 3 weeks ago, available until 7:33am on 9 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves Enid Brighton

Duration: 7 minutes 7m

Pug wants to write exciting stories and our two friends meet Enid Brighton. She has a very vivid imagination and they go an adventure to solve all of her mysteries.

This episode was published 3 weeks ago, available until 7:33am on 8 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves Camping

Duration: 7 minutes 7m

Dog and Pug decide to go holiday and use Granny Pug's guide to camping. They visit a music festival, a jungle and they go to the top of a mountain although are very happy to return to the bookshop.

This episode was published 3 weeks ago, available until 7:33am on 7 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves Cheshire Cat

Duration: 7 minutes 7m

Dog and Pug go to Buckingham Palace where they meet Queen Victoria. She is looking for her mysterious Cheshire Cat, who keeps disappearing. Pug tempts him out with a saucer of cream.

This episode was published 3 weeks ago, available until 7:33am on 6 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves Bedtime Baboon

Duration: 7 minutes 7m

Dog and Pug go on an adventure to help Bedtime Baboon get all the young children around the world to sleep. They sprinkle moondust everywhere to help them sleep, however when they run out of moondust, they visit the moon.

This episode was published 4 weeks ago, available until 7:33am on 5 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves Puzzles

Duration: 7 minutes 7m

In their adventure, Dog and Pug meet Granny Pug who makes them solve three puzzles. We see Dog and Pug solving all Granny's puzzles, just in time. Dog comes back to the bookshop and solves his own problem.

This episode was published 4 weeks ago, available until 7:33am on 4 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves Elephants

Duration: 7 minutes 7m

This adventure is all about size. Elephant and a mongoose cannot play together because they are two completely difference sizes. Dog and Pug solve the problem by helping them work together.

This episode was published 4 weeks ago, available until 7:32am on 3 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves King Arthur And The Round Table

Duration: 7 minutes 7m

Dog and Pug visit King Arthur in his castle, where he and his knights shout at each other at their Long Table. With the help of Merlin the magician they change to a Round Table so they can hear each other speak.

This episode was published 4 weeks ago, available until 7:33am on 2 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves Calm

LAST CHANCE
Duration: 7 minutes 7m

Dog and Pug escape a very noisy Bookshop to find Granny Pug who is leading a mediation retreat. They try to find a peaceful spot but return to the bookshop where Dog can make some noise.

This episode was published 4 weeks ago, available until 7:33am on 1 Mar 2022.

Dog Loves Fairy Tales

LAST CHANCE
Duration: 7 minutes 7m

Dog and Pug meet Little Red Riding Hood and confuse the Big Bad Wolf by fooling him into chasing them and making him try to blow the Three Little Pigs brick house down.

This episode was published 4 weeks ago. Available for another 7 hours.
