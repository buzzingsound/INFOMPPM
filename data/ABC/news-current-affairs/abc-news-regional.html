Miriam Corowa brings you a closer look at what's making headlines in regional and rural Australia. ABC News reporters across the country uncover the news and unique stories about what's really going on outside the big cities.

Saturdays 12.30pm AEDT on ABC iview and ABC NEWS.

Episodes

Saturday 19/2/2022

Duration: 28 minutes 28m

Australia's largest coal fired power plant to shut in just 3 years, plus graziers in the Gulf of Carpentaria hope for a late season deluge, and exclusive talks for regional Victoria to host the 2026 Commonwealth Games.

This episode was published 7 days ago, available until 12:30pm on 21 Mar 2022.

Saturday 12/2/2022

Duration: 28 minutes 28m

Duration: 28 minutes 28m

Cut off and running out of supplies huge parts of central Australia inundated. Whats being done to support remote communities of the APY lands. Plus racism, sexism and allegations of rape at one of the world's biggest miners.

This episode was published 3 weeks ago, available until 12:30pm on 7 Mar 2022.

Saturday 29/1/2022

Duration: 27 minutes 27m

Heavy rain hits northern Queensland and Victoria, while central Australia is still assessing the damage of last week's floods. Plus residents of one NSW town band together to buy the local pub. Presented by Jeremy Fernandez.
