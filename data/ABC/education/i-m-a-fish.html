I'm A Fish

ABC MEEDUCATION

I'm a Fish features a variety of sea creatures who invite us into their underwater world. They tell us all sorts of fun and interesting facts about where they live, what they do and how they survive in such deep places! Early Stage 1 - Stage 1 Science and Technology.
