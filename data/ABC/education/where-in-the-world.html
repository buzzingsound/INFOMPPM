Follow the everyday lives of children living in eight different countries around the world, speaking a different language and having different customs, discoveries, thrills and challenges. Early Stage 1 - Stage 1 HSIE.

Series 1

Episode 1 Tyra, Hamza and Liza. Makes Blueberry Pancakes

Duration: 14 minutes 14 seconds14m

In Russia, Liza picks blueberries with her grandma; in Ghana Tyra goes to the market with her grandma and in Jordan, Hamza has a day out in town with his dad.

This episode was published 3 years ago.

Episode 2 Tyra, Mario and Anushka. Goes Shopping for Eggs

Duration: 14 minutes 13 seconds14m

In Ghana,Tyra is scrubbed from head to toe by her mum for church; in India Anushka goes shopping for eggs with her grandad and in Italy, Mario is put through a gruelling workout at his kick-boxing class.

This episode was published 3 years ago.

Episode 3 Mario, Anushka and Hamza. Makes a Kite

Duration: 14 minutes 7 seconds14m

Duration: 14 minutes 13 seconds14m

Duration: 14 minutes 10 seconds14m

Duration: 14 minutes 3 seconds14m

Duration: 14 minutes 13 seconds14m

Duration: 14 minutes 4 seconds14m

In Jordan, Hamza makes lunch with his mum; in Vietnam, Truc makes paper butterflies and wins a relay race. And in Italy Mario, his brother and sister spend the day at their grandma's house, dressing up and eating ice cream.

Duration: 14 minutes 14 seconds14m

In Peru, Angel plays football with his cousins - once he has finished his chores, in Canada, Rhys plays games in school where it's warm, and outside in the chilly snow. And in Ghana, Tyra helps her mum cook lunch.

Duration: 14 minutes 12 seconds14m

Duration: 14 minutes 11 seconds14m

Duration: 14 minutes 2 seconds14m

Duration: 14 minutes 15 seconds14m

In Ghana Tyra learns English at school, in Russia Liza spends the morning at the playground trying to beat her twin brother at standing on one leg. And in Italy, Mario sings a song in front of a whole choir.
