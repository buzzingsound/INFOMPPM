Space Nova

ABC MEFAMILY

In the year 2161, the Nova family embark on an action-packed search for extra-terrestrial intelligence. Their quest will bring them face-to-face with epic life-forms and strange worlds beyond their wildest dreams.

Series 1

Episode 1 Star Dust

Duration: 23 minutes 23m

The Novas, a family of space explorers make an interstellar discovery that will change the course of history when they chance across an alien space craft and its ethereal substance - Star Dust.

This episode was published 7 months ago.

Episode 2 Over Being A Nova

Duration: 23 minutes 23m

Bored with cataloguing rocks, Adelaide accepts a challenge to a "space-race" against Sol. But when Sol gets in trouble, Adelaide and Jet perform a heroic rescue and rediscover what being a Nova is all about.

This episode was published 7 months ago.

Episode 3 Black Hamster

Duration: 23 minutes 23m

When Jet creates a nutrient-dense 'super pickle', it attracts a space hamster whose survival instinct puts the whole port in great danger. Jet will have to free his new friend in order to guarantee Luna Port's safety.

This episode was published 7 months ago.

Episode 4 Escape From TR-227

Duration: 23 minutes 23m

After crash landing while on a supply run to a research planet, the Novas come face-to-face with green-skinned natives and a deadly gravity storm that threatens not only their lives but some life-saving research!

This episode was published 7 months ago.

Episode 5 Seaweed Samba

Duration: 23 minutes 23m

The Novas travel to a suspected Star Dust Planet, but instead find themselves trapped underwater by a sentient seaweed plant. Meanwhile, Aubrina sends Sol out undercover to learn what the Novas are up to.

This episode was published 7 months ago.

Episode 6 Steel Comet

Duration: 23 minutes 23m

When a large comet appears on a collision course with Luna Port, the Novas take a huge risk to divert it, only to realise it's no comet at all, but rather a frozen spaceship presumed lost for nearly 100 years!

This episode was published 7 months ago.

Episode 7 Friends In Dark Places

Duration: 23 minutes 23m

Continuing their search for Star Dust aliens, the Novas discover an uninhabited planet - but when they fall through quicksand to the caverns beneath, they soon discover it isn't uninhabited at all!

This episode was published 7 months ago.

Episode 8 Invisible Siblings

Duration: 23 minutes 23m

After G9 malfunctions at a Luna Port celebration, Aubrina seeks to reprogram him. Jet and Adelaide go on a mission to save their robot only to find Aubrina has some secrets of her own!

This episode was published 7 months ago.

Episode 9 Steam Ship

Duration: 23 minutes 23m

After the Novas are forced to land on a junk planet for repairs, they come face-to-face with Old Phil and his dog Calisto. Phil claims that he enjoys solitude and quiet, but he may know more than he is letting on!

This episode was published 7 months ago.

Episode 10 Grafter

Duration: 23 minutes 23m

Sol attempts to win the science fair by sabotaging all the other projects including Jet and Adelaide's half plant, half animal. But Sol's actions cause chaos and reveal an alien may have been to Luna Port!

This episode was published 7 months ago.

Episode 11 Ghost Station

Duration: 23 minutes 23m

Josie surprises the Novas by booking a 'ghost tour' of a spooky space station. But when they receive a distress call from the station, they realise this ghost tour may be more real than they bargained for!

This episode was published 7 months ago.

Episode 12 The Jump

Duration: 23 minutes 23m

The Novas expose Aubrina's wrongdoing and the Star Dust Crystal is returned. They're ready to make their first Star Dust 'jump' and explore unknown parts of space but Aubrina enlists a shadowy figure to stop the Novas!

This episode was published 7 months ago.

Episode 13 Finally An Alien

Duration: 23 minutes 23m

The Novas are now able to jump millions of lightyears and their quest to find Star Dust Aliens kicks into high gear. But on their first trip, Jet and Adelaide realise that an alien might be closer to home than they realised.

This episode was published 7 months ago.

Episode 16 The Puzzle

Duration: 23 minutes 23m

The Novas investigate a seemingly deserted planet, millions of lightyears away with pyramid-like temples. Soon, however, they find the planet isn't deserted, and the inhabitants aren't the type looking for a friendly chat!

This episode was published 5 months ago.

Episode 17 A Black Hole Ate My Homework

Duration: 23 minutes 23m

When Jet and Adelaide realise they have too much work to get through in one night, they devise a plan to take Adelaide's flyer to the very edge of a black hole in order to slow down time.

This episode was published 5 months ago.

Episode 18 White Hamster

Duration: 23 minutes 23m

The Novas discover the escape pod that Jet used to evacuate Pickles the space hamster into uncharted space. When they finally catch up with Pickles however, they find he and his family need their help more than ever.

This episode was published 5 months ago.

Episode 19 Pamela Barnacle Is Alive and Well

Duration: 23 minutes 23m

Luna Port's security system is malfunctioning, so the Novas visit Pamela Barnacle, the system's creator at her private asteroid. But when they arrive they find there's more than meets the eye with Pamela and her creation.

This episode was published 5 months ago.

Episode 20 Shore Leave

Duration: 23 minutes 23m

The Novas spot a planet that looks like paradise so they decide to visit for some much-needed R&R. Soon, however, their dream getaway turns into a nightmare - and one that they're unable to wake up from!

This episode was published 5 months ago.

Episode 21 Alienosaurs

Duration: 23 minutes 23m

The Novas head to an Earth-like planet, hoping to find the Star Dust Aliens. Instead, they find highly evolved and aggressive Alien dinosaurs, and discover what it's like when Humans are at the bottom of the food chain!

This episode was published 5 months ago.

Episode 22 Collision Course

Duration: 23 minutes 23m

In the middle of a grav-ball game against their arch-rivals, Luna Port faces a catastrophic shut-down and blackout. It's now up to Jet, Adelaide and Sol to get the port back online before it crashes into an asteroid belt!

This episode was published 5 months ago.

Episode 23 Yowie!

Duration: 23 minutes 23m

One of the Novas' drones delivers footage that will make them question everything they know about alien life; a blurry video of what looks like a real-life bigfoot on a planet millions of light years from Earth!

This episode was published 5 months ago.

Episode 24 Sweet Dream

Duration: 23 minutes 23m

Sol has vivid dreams about his home world, so the Novas enlist the help of their 'energy creature' friend to uncover details of his planet. But things take a turn when Sol's brain refuses to release him from the dream state!

This episode was published 5 months ago.

Episode 25 Dark Ice

Duration: 23 minutes 23m

The Novas journey to Sol's home planet - only to discover that it is completely frozen over. But they soon realise the planet may yet hold the answers to all their questions - but first they must journey below the ice.

This episode was published 5 months ago.

Episode 26 Star Dust Aliens

Duration: 23 minutes 23m

The Novas locate another Alien rock ship and set off to make contact. But they're intercepted by Aubrina and left stranded. Will the Novas lose their chance to make real contact with the Star Dust Aliens forever?

This episode was published 5 months ago.

Episode 14 The Goldilocks Zone

Duration: 23 minutes 23m

The Novas and Sol begin their search for Sol's home planet. Their first stop leads them on a mission to a planet inhabited entirely by mega-flora - only for Sol to go missing.

This episode was published 5 months ago.

Episode 15 Rogue Planet

Duration: 23 minutes 23m

When the Novas and Sol go to investigate a potential Star Dust Ship - they get trapped on a rogue planet heading directly towards the system's sun! Meanwhile, Aubrina has plans to get her own Star Dust crystal.

This episode was published 5 months ago.
