Matt and Zindzi are back in the Art Time studio providing joyful inspiration for little artists! This series focuses on what can be found at home and feeling inspired by what's around you.

Episodes

3D Wild Animals

Duration: 9 minutes 48 seconds9m

Put your empty pizza boxes to good use! Be transported to the African Savanna in this episode of Art Time, as Zindzi recycles materials to make sculptures of a giraffe and a lion within a cardboard pizza-box diorama!

This episode was published 5 months ago.

Weaving

Duration: 8 minutes 36 seconds8m

In this episode of Art Time, Matt is demonstrating the fascinating textile art of weaving! Matt uses lots of different warm and cool colours to weave some wonderful woollen artworks to hang in the Art Time Gallery.

This episode was published 5 months ago.

Upcycled Printmaking

Duration: 8 minutes 49 seconds8m

Zindzi is gathering a little of this and that to upcycle for this episode of Art Time. Join her in the Art Time studio turning trash to treasure and learn how we can reuse things like sponges and lids to do printmaking!

This episode was published 5 months ago.

Shadow Play

Duration: 7 minutes 57 seconds7m

Matt is back to light up the Art Time studio and experiment with making art using shadows! Matt creates a craft model of the city and countryside that when lit by a torch make for some fun and interesting shadow play.

This episode was published 5 months ago.

Imaginary Worlds Collage

Duration: 7 minutes 37 seconds7m

This episode of Art Time is out of this world! Join Zindzi having fun recycling magazines and newspapers in the Art Time studio, using her imagination to create collages of fun worlds to display in the Art Time Gallery.

This episode was published 5 months ago.

Moving Art

Duration: 7 minutes 52 seconds7m

Get moving and making with Matt in the Art Time studio! Matt is revisiting previous episodes to repurpose his family figurines and shape jars, using these to create fun animations to bring a whole new life to his artworks.

This episode was published 5 months ago.

Art Ideas Book

Duration: 9 minutes 8 seconds9m

Zindzi has so many fun and creative ideas for artworks almost too many to remember! Join Zindzi in the Art Time studio as she sketches and scrapbooks her inspirations and ideas into an Art Ideas Book. What a great idea!

This episode was published 5 months ago.

Laundry Landscape

Duration: 9 minutes 7 seconds9m

Matt is going on an adventure, but he has some laundry to do first Join him in the Art Time studio as he creates imaginative landscapes of the jungle, desert and mountains using laundry items. Laundry has never been so fun!

This episode was published 5 months ago.

Watercolour Skies

Duration: 7 minutes 24 seconds7m

Get your watercolours and imaginations ready the sky is the limit! Zindzi is feeling inspired by the spectacular skies. Using watercolours, she paints some different colourful skies to showcase in the Art Time Gallery.

This episode was published 5 months ago.

Family Portrait

Duration: 8 minutes 53 seconds8m

Join Matt in the Art Time studio and be introduced to some members of his family in photographs! Matt makes some fun craft characters using recycled materials to represent his family and create a family portrait.

This episode was published 5 months ago.

Stick And Nature Hanging

Duration: 9 minutes 14 seconds9m

Zindzi has been out and about collecting things from nature to include in her art. Join her in the Art Time studio as she uses natural materials like sticks and pine cones to build a colourful wall hanging inspired by nature!

This episode was published 5 months ago.

Shape Jar

Duration: 7 minutes 41 seconds7m

In this episode of Art Time we discover fun and dynamic shapes and how to include them in art! Join Matt as he uses a Shape Jar to make interesting artworks from all kinds of shapes to go on display in the Art Time Gallery.

This episode was published 5 months ago.

Boats

Duration: 11 minutes 50 seconds11m

Ahoy! The Art Crew are creating all types of different boats to set sail in the Art Time Gallery. Zindzi also makes her own boat with a beautiful fabric sail and then makes a stunning glittering lighthouse out of fabric.

This episode was published 7 months ago.

Mini Beasts

Duration: 9 minutes 55 seconds9m

Matt and the Art crew have some special little mini beast visitors in the Art Room today! They create their own buggy world using clay, leaves and nature craft. Matt makes a bee and a clay coil hive for it to live in.

This episode was published 7 months ago.

Paper World

Duration: 10 minutes 50 seconds10m

Zindzi and the Art Crew are inspired by works by Paper Engineer Benja Harney as they make their own giant paper people. Zindzi makes a colourful paper cloud and a paper plant.

This episode was published 7 months ago.

Dino Swamp

Duration: 11 minutes 23 seconds11m

Were stomping with Dinosaurs on Art Time today as Matt and The Art Crew create dinosaurs out of colourful dough. Matt then constructs a 3D diorama complete with a volcano for them to live in as they come to life.

This episode was published 7 months ago.

Still Life

Duration: 10 minutes 23 seconds10m

Inspired by a trip to the local Market, Matt has brought back fruit and flowers for the Art Crew to create a still life painting. They look at colour and composition in works by Van Gough and Cezanne still life paintings.

Down on the Farm

Duration: 11 minutes 28 seconds11m

Were heading down to the farm in this episode as Matt and the Art Crew create tractors and farm animals out of recycled boxes. Matt brings his tractor to life using stop motion.

Rainforest

City and Train

Duration: 12 minutes 12 seconds12m

Inspired by interesting buildings in the city Matt and the Art Crew are creating a city scape with blocks and tape. Matt constructs a train to take people around which all comes together with a song in the Art Time Gallery!

Lost and Found

Duration: 11 minutes 19 seconds11m

The Art Crew are bringing in unwanted objects from home which will ignite our imaginations for storytelling with art. They then reuse the photos to create a colourful collaborative project with all the colours of the rainbow!

This episode was published 7 months ago.

Monsters

Matt and The Art Crew are making silly bottle monsters for Art Time today out of all kind of recycled materials. Join Matt for a hilarious song with the finished creatures in the Art Time Gallery.

Portraits

Duration: 12 minutes 4 seconds12m

Zindzi and the Art Crew warm up their faces with a dancing face song and are all ready to paint self-portraits! Zindzi also makes a colourful cardboard mask to be celebrated in the Art Time Gallery.

This episode was published 7 months ago.

Birds

Duration: 11 minutes 42 seconds11m

Matt and the Art Crew are creating all sorts of birds by drawing and using colourful tissue paper. Matt then makes a large tree for them to live in, which is celebrated with a song in the Art Time Gallery.

Puppets

Duration: 12 minutes 8 seconds12m

Zindzi and the Art crew create puppets using all kinds of kitchen utensils and fabric.The Art Crew then put on a special puppet show in the Art Time Gallery!

Under the Sea

Duration: 10 minutes 43 seconds10m

Matt and the Art Crew discover styro print making, inspired by fish and other sea creatures. Matt finds an interesting way to print on fabric using rolling pins to create a wonderful underwater world in the Art Time Gallery!

Patterns

Duration: 10 minutes 35 seconds10m

Zindzi and the Art Crew get inspired from a painting by Bronwyn Bancroft and create their own landscape making patterns in paint. Zindzi then makes a beautiful full moon using natural materials found in the bush.

Water Lilies

Duration: 12 minutes 3 seconds12m

Matt and the Art crew are inspired by Monet's beautiful water lilies and get busy with a collaborative painting. Next they create colourful lily pads and Matt makes a craft frog who enjoys a new pond in the Art Time Gallery!

Robots

Australian Animals

Space
