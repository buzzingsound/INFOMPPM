Get comfy on the Story Time couch as Australian celebrities read an amazing range of children's books. Don't miss Layne Beachley, Miguel Maestre, Dr Karl, Simon Burke and many more!

Layne Beachley: Magic Beach

Duration: 6 minutes 32 seconds6m

Get ready for a whale of a time in this magical episode! Join professional surfer Layne Beachley and her pal Humpty, reading a true Australian classic, Magic Beach by Alison Lester.
